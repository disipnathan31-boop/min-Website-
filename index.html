About MEA

MEA is a nonprofit organization made up of educated individuals from the Min ethnic group in Papua New Guinea.

Where We Work

Faiwolmin, Olsobip, Tabubil, Kiunga, Telefomin, Oksapmin, Tifalmin and surrounding areas.

Vision 2030

To empower Min communities into a smart, fair, healthy and happy society.

Mission

To unite, educate, and empower Min elites to transform communities through leadership and integrity.
